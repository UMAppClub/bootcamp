Welcome to App Club

App Club is the tech community at University of Malaya.
We learn, teach, build, and grow as a team.

Learn

Inspired by hackathons' model of motivation, productivity and creativity, we founded The App Club in 2016 to learn new technologies and tools together, more frequently. The question we were facing was: "We've been to 2 hackathons. We've built 2 products in 48 hours, and 0 products in the rest of the year. What's going wrong?". The answer was obvious, we needed a different learning platform. There wasn't one, so we thought we'd create one!

App Club mission

Build

We're running a 10-week bootcamp from February to May 2018 to accelerate our learning/building process. 8 teams will get the tech mentorship and education they need to execute and launch their app ideas in campus (or beyond).

Learn more

Disrupt

Our goal is to build the tech entrepreneurship ecosystem in University of Malaya, by building a culture of "try fast, fail fast". We aim to maximize sharing, minimize competition and inspire students to work to be the best they can possibly be.

Ask us

"If you are spending more time getting good grades than learning stuff, you need to start worrying about your education!!!"

Improving our tech education through industrial mentorship

Technology is growing fast. Too fast, perhaps, for universities to catch up. Therefore at App Club we've been directly getting insights and inspiration from our mentors from tech scene in Malaysia and abroad to constantly improve our definition of right and wrong. Our mentors have guided us since the beginning, from founding the club to growing it and making it work beyond UM.

Community engagement

We are happy to invite passionate community developers or tech enthusiasts to share their knowledge with us, growing our knowledge and their network.

By students, for students

We're a group of CS undergrads at UM who wish to push the community's standards higher, and encourage sharing of knowledge and opportunities, unconditionally.
